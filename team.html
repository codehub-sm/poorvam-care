Ms.Apoorva Rai

MASLP, Clinical Director

Our team is led by Apoorva Rai, MASLP who is licensed by the Rehabilitation Council of India and Indian Speech and Hearing Association, and has been a therapist over 7 years. She has a wealth of paediatric experience working with children with Cochlear implant, ASD, learning disabilities, ADHD, developmental delays, and Articulation Disorders. Her work as a speech therapist is driven by her passion for success by combining sensory-based strategies, Play based therapy and behavioural techniques in order to obtain maximum results. She has worked in hospitals, clinics and private schools with national and international organizations. She is a strong advocate for early intervention and has devoted her career to improving the lives of children with communication disorders. She is passionate about inspiring parents, children, and her team members to keep exploring new ways to foster their Knowledge, attitude and skill.

Mr.Padmaraj

Consultant Occupational Therapist

He is a paediatric occupational therapist with nearly 16 years of experience. His expertise is working with children having ASD, learning disabilities, and developmental delays. His approach is holistic, developmental, and relationship based. He understands that creating trust with children and families is the foremost important step of any therapy program and she strives to achieve emotional engagement with all of her clients. He has worked with multidisciplinary teams on assessments and implementation of behavioural intervention and skill acquisition programs.
